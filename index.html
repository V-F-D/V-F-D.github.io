Frequently Asked Questions

What types of clothing do you offer?

We offer a variety of ready-made clothes, custom-made clothing tailored to your specifications, and repair services for all types of garments.

How can I place a custom order?

To place a custom order, you can visit our store or contact us via phone. Our designers will work with you to create the perfect piece.

Do you offer repair services?

Yes, we offer a wide range of repair services for all types of clothing. Whether you need a simple hem adjustment or a complete garment overhaul, our experienced tailors are here to help.

What training courses do you offer?

We offer comprehensive training courses in fashion design and tailoring. Our courses are designed for beginners as well as advanced learners who want to refine their skills.

How can I enroll in a training course?

To enroll in one of our training courses, you can visit our training academy, contact us via phone, or register through our website. Our team will provide you with all the necessary details and support.

What are your business hours?

Our business hours are Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.
